about rocks

Rocks is a fast paced infinite runner set in the year 3084. Mankind has escaped its native solar-system in search of new resources and new worlds. Due to the lack of 'shit to do' in space, pilots from the military, mining and racing industries compete to be the most fearless by dodging and shooting their way through the most dangerous asteroid fields in the galaxy.
