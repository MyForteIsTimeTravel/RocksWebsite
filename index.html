about rocks

Rocks is a fast paced infinite runner set in the year 3084. Mankind has escaped its native solar-system in search of new resources and new worlds. Due to the lack of 'shit to do' in space, pilots from the military, industrial-mining and racing backgrounds compete to be the best in the underground asteroid chicken-type deal.
